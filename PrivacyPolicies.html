Privacy Policies

Information We Collect
  • Personal Information: Such as your name, contact details, date of birth, and medical history when you register or place an order.
  • Prescription Information: When you upload prescriptions for medication orders.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with our website, including IP address, device information, and browsing behavior.
  • Cookies and Tracking Technologies: Details on how we use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience and gather information.
How We Use Your Information
  • Providing Services: Managing your medication orders, processing payments, and delivering medications to your doorstep.
  • Prescription Review: Verifying prescriptions uploaded to ensure accurate dispensing.
  • Communication: Sending order confirmations, updates, and customer service responses.
  • Improving Services: Analyzing user behavior to enhance our website's functionality and user experience.
  • Legal Compliance: Fulfilling legal and regulatory obligations.
Information Sharing and Disclosure
  • Service Providers: Sharing information with trusted partners who help us operate our services.
  • Legal Requirements: Disclosing information as required by law, regulation, or legal process.
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred.
  • Consent: Sharing information with your explicit consent.
Your Choices
  • Account Information: You can review and update your account details at any time.
  • Communications: You can manage your communication preferences.
  • Cookies: You can manage cookies and tracking technologies through your browser settings.
Data Security
  • We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.
  • However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.
Children's Privacy
  • Our services are intended for users above a certain age, as specified in our Terms of Service. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under this age.
Updates to this Privacy Policy
  • We may update this privacy policy to reflect changes in our practices or for legal reasons. We will notify you of significant changes through prominent notices on our website.
